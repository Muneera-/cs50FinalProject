Here's a little information about ourselves!

Born out of the love for 80's and 90's culture nostalgia, as well as the desire to expose people to the arts outside of mainstream media culture, Bob's Cool Videos has been dedicated to helping its customers find the perfect videos that they want while at the same expose them to different genres of film throughout the ages.

Small, cozy, and situated in the heart of Memphis, Tennessee, this beloved gem has attracted millions of independent filmmakers, as well as tourists, to the front of its doors since 2008.

Because we want our customers to feel valued for their services, for every purchase that you make on a VCR, Blueray, and/or DVD, a third of the proceeds goes to the reconstruction of the Black Lodge, which has for so long been the place for independent filmmakers in the Memphis region to display their masterpieces to the entire world.

Disclaimer: Even though we perfectly understand that not everyone uses these devices anymore, we still want to purchase one of our beloved antiques/"pieces of old crap" so that you may share them with the rest of the world and become another wannabe hipster like the rest of us posers! You never know, one of those tapes might be worth at least a million dollars in the long run. ;)
